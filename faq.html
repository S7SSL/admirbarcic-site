Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to the questions clients, journalists and partners most often ask about Admir Barčić — his background, the services he provides, the regions he covers, and how to make contact.

Who is Admir Barčić?

Admir Barčić (also written Admir Barcic) is a security and risk management director with 28+ years of field experience across Iraq, Afghanistan, Libya, South Sudan, Nigeria and Ukraine. He is International Business Development Manager at Evolution Global Risk Management, a Board Member of the International Police Organization (IPO HQ) and a certified PMC instructor. He is based in Slovenia and Montenegro and available internationally.

What services does Admir Barčić provide?

He provides personnel repatriation, close protection, crisis extraction, security and threat assessment, secure escort and transfer, and multi-jurisdictional logistics coordination for individuals, corporations, NGOs and governments operating in high-risk environments. See the capabilities page for detail on each service.

Where is Admir Barčić based and which regions does he cover?

He is based in Slovenia and Montenegro and operates worldwide, with particular depth across the United Kingdom, the Gulf/GCC region, the Balkans, Eastern Europe, the Middle East and Africa. He is available internationally at short notice.

What languages does Admir Barčić speak?

English, Slovenian and Bosnian at working level.

What major operations has Admir Barčić been involved in?

He played an operational role in the 2021–2022 Afghanistan evacuations (with partners including Mercury One and the Nazarene Fund), the 2026 Gulf crisis evacuation of over 900 Slovenian citizens recognised by the Government of Slovenia, humanitarian relocation through Montenegro, and crisis-response operations connected to the conflict in Ukraine. The full operations record sets these out in detail.

How quickly can Admir Barčić respond to a crisis or extraction request?

Time from activation to wheels-up is typically measured in hours, not days. For urgent or time-sensitive requests, direct email to ab@admirbarcic.com is preferred.

Does Admir Barčić work with governments and NGOs as well as private clients?

Yes. He works with governments, international organisations, NGOs, corporations, high-net-worth individuals and diplomats. All engagements are handled with strict confidentiality.

How do you contact Admir Barčić?

Email ab@admirbarcic.com. Enquiries are handled confidentially, typically with a response within 24 hours.
