Who operates this site

admirbarcic.com is operated by Admir Barčić, a self-employed security and risk management consultant based in Slovenia and Montenegro.

What we collect

When you email ab@admirbarcic.com we receive and store the content of your message, including your name, email address, and any operational details you choose to share. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry.

Cookies and analytics

With your explicit consent (Accept on the cookie banner) we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site. No analytics cookie is set unless you click Accept. You can change your choice at any time by clearing your browser's site data for admirbarcic.com.

Who we share data with

Enquiry content is never shared with third parties. If we engage you as a client, any operational details shared during the engagement are treated as strictly confidential and handled in accordance with the applicable NDA and professional discretion standards.

Retention

Emails are retained only as long as needed to respond to and, where applicable, deliver the enquiry. You may request deletion at any time by emailing ab@admirbarcic.com.

Your rights

Under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR you have rights of access, correction, deletion and portability over any personal data we hold about you. Contact ab@admirbarcic.com to exercise any of these.
