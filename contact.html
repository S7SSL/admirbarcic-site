What to include in your enquiry

To scope a response quickly, include as much of the following as you can share at this stage:

  • Type of engagement (repatriation, close protection, assessment, extraction, escort, logistics).
  • Geographic region(s) involved.
  • Timeline — immediate, days, weeks.
  • Nature of the principal or personnel (corporate, diplomatic, family, witness, etc.).
  • Any constraints (budget range, jurisdiction restrictions, secrecy level).

Full details are not needed in the first message. Rough contours are enough to decide whether we can help and to set up a confidential call.
