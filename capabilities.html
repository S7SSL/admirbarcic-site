What I Do

Capabilities

Specialist services for individuals, organisations, and governments that need trusted operational support at short notice. Each engagement is scoped around the specific threat, jurisdiction, and timeline — not a fixed package.

Personnel Repatriation

Safe and discreet repatriation of personnel from high-risk regions, conflict zones, and complex jurisdictions. End-to-end logistics managed: documentation, ground transport, secure accommodation through transit, aviation coordination, and arrival-country handover. Past operations include mass evacuations from Afghanistan (2021–2022) and the Gulf region (2026).

Close Protection

Personal protection for executives, high-net-worth individuals, diplomats, and private clients. Domestic and international assignments, single-principal and team deployments. Short-notice availability for travel into elevated-risk environments. All operatives trained to PMC-instructor standard.

Crisis Extraction

Rapid extraction of personnel from deteriorating situations — conflict escalation, civil unrest, infrastructure collapse, health emergencies. Experience coordinating evacuations across the Gulf, Eastern Europe, and the wider Middle East and Africa. Time from activation to wheels-up typically measured in hours, not days.

Security Assessment

Pre-travel and destination security assessments for corporate, diplomatic, and private travel into elevated-risk environments. Includes threat analysis, route planning, accommodation vetting, communications protocols, and contingency procedures tailored to the specific traveller and destination.

Secure Escort and Transfer

Discreet escort and transfer services for individuals requiring safe passage across borders or through complex operational environments. Suitable for at-risk individuals, witnesses, family members being relocated, and clients moving through hostile or contested regions.

Logistics and Multi-jurisdictional Coordination

Ground-level coordination for complex multi-jurisdictional operations. Local networks across six former operating theatres, documentation and permits handling, liaison with host-country authorities and international organisations, establishment of transit hubs. The work that makes the rest of the work possible.
