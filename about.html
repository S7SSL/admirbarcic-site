Background

28 Years. Six Conflict Zones.

Admir Barčić has spent the last twenty-eight years inside active conflict zones, crisis response theatres, and multi-jurisdictional security operations — as both operative and strategist. His current work is advisory, but his field experience is what clients are buying.

Current roles

  • International Business Development Manager at Evolution Global Risk Management (EGRM) — global risk consulting serving governments, international organisations and private clients.
  • Board Member at the International Police Organization (IPO HQ).
  • Certified PMC Instructor — private military contractor training at practitioner level.

Operational history

Field experience spans six active conflict zones across three decades: Iraq, Afghanistan, Libya, South Sudan, Nigeria, and Ukraine. The work has covered close protection, hostile-environment operations, humanitarian corridors, and civilian evacuation under active threat.

Previous senior roles

  • Global Director at SECU, Riyadh — senior security leadership for Gulf-region operations.
  • Global Business Development Director at Stratex Group — 14+ years across Europe and the Gulf, building and leading client-facing security delivery.

Base and availability

Based in Slovenia and Montenegro. Available internationally at short notice for direct client work, advisory engagements, and operational consultation. All engagements handled with absolute discretion — client names, locations, and operational details are not published.
